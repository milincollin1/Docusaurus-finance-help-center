# Building a T-Bill Ladder

1. Determine your monthly income goal (e.g. $2,000/mo).  
2. Break into T-bills of staggered maturities (4w, 8w, 13w, 26w).  
3. Reinvest proceeds into the longest rung.

### Sample Ladder

| Maturity | Amount |
| -------- | ------ |
| 4 weeks  | $5,000 |
| 8 weeks  | $5,000 |
| 13 weeks | $5,000 |
| 26 weeks | $5,000 |
